Take a Trip with Us!

Carson River

Take an exciting 2 hour trip down the Carson River.
The trip will start in Pimsdale at the Idaho launch area.
From there, it is a leisure trip down the river to the
Steelbarn launch area.
There are plenty of places to pull out and take a break or
have a snack.

This trip is an easy, beginner friendly trip.


This trip is rated Class I

American River

This is a challenging 4 hour trip down the American River.
The trip will start outside Larsen at the Reina launch area.
From there, there are many stretches of challenging rapids
interspersed with calmer waters.
The trip will end at the Glory Falls launch area.
This run will really challenge you and your rafting skills.



This trip is rated Class IV

Kings River

This is a moderately challenging 3 hour trip down the Kings River.
The trip will start outside Kantucket at the Buffalo launch area.
From there, there are few areas of fun rapids that require some
skill to navigate. There are many stretches of calm water to relax
and enjoy the scenary as well.
The trip will end at the Samuel Dip launch area.
This run is moderately challenging with technical skill required.



This trip is rated Class III
